The Baker
"No culinary school. No mentor. Just Greg, a 5-year-old starter named Gloria, and an unhealthy obsession with fermentation science."

What started as a lockdown experiment became a manifesto. G-Spot is where the long, slow magic of wild yeast meets the bold, unapologetic flavors of the Indian subcontinent. Every loaf, every pie — a product of time, temperature, and a little bit of chaos.

That chaos lives in Gloria — a five-year-old sourdough starter fed daily, never rushed, with strong opinions about hydration ratios. She's the heart of everything baked at G-Spot.

Self-taught and obsession-driven, Greg brings South Asian flavors into artisan sourdough and pizza in ways nobody else was doing. Achari paneer on a sourdough crust fired at 450°C felt like the most obvious idea in the world. So he moved from a Brooklyn apartment to Greenwich Village and made it permanent.

Sourcing flour from Farmer Ground Flour, Hudson Valley. Tomatoes from DiNardo Family Farms, NJ.
